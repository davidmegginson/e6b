E6B practice

This is a free mini-app from David Megginson, for fellow pilots who love the classic E6B circular sliderule, or for student pilots still forced to learn it.

You can get practice exercises for either the wind side or the calculator side. Just keep clicking/tapping/hitting a key to see the answer, then to move on to the next question.
